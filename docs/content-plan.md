## 5. Service pages  (one page each — shared template below)

> Template for every service page:
> 1. Hero (eyebrow + H1 outcome headline + sub + CTA)
> 2. "Is this you?" — 3–4 pain-point bullets
> 3. What we do — 3–6 feature/benefit blocks
> 4. How it works — 3–4 step process
> 5. Deliverables / what you get
> 6. Pricing approach or "Get a quote"
> 7. Related work / mini case study
> 8. FAQ (4–6 Q&As — great for SEO)
> 9. CTA band

### 5.1 Website Development
- H1: **A website that works as hard as you do.**
- Sub: Fast, modern, mobile-first websites for any business — from a clean one-pager to a full e-commerce store.
- Pain points: slow/outdated site · doesn't show up on Google · no enquiries · can't update it yourself.
- What we do: Business websites · Landing pages · E-commerce · Web apps/portals · Redesigns · Maintenance.
- FAQ seeds: "How long does a website take?" · "Will I be able to edit it myself?" · "Do you do SEO?" · "What does it cost?"

### 5.2 ERP & Management Systems  (hub page → links to industry pages)
- H1: **One system to run your entire operation.**
- Sub: Custom ERP and management software built around your domain — schools, clinics, retail, manufacturing and more. Admissions, billing, inventory, reports — all in one place.
- What we do: Custom ERP · Inventory · Billing & GST · CRM · Reporting dashboards · Role-based access.
- "Built for your domain" grid → School ERP · Clinic Management · Retail · Manufacturing · Custom.
- FAQ: "Can it replace our current software?" · "Is our data safe?" · "Can staff learn it easily?" · "Do you offer support?"

### 5.3 AI & Automation
- H1: **Put the boring, repetitive work on autopilot.**
- Sub: WhatsApp bots, AI chat & calling agents, and automated workflows that save hours every day — built with n8n and OpenAI.
- What we do: WhatsApp/chatbots · AI calling agents · Workflow automation (n8n) · Auto-reports · Lead routing · Document processing.
- FAQ: "What can be automated?" · "Will it integrate with my tools?" · "Is it expensive to run?"

### 5.4 Performance Marketing
- H1: **Ads that bring in customers, not just clicks.**
- Sub: Google and Meta campaigns managed for ROI — we track every rupee from ad to enquiry to sale.
- What we do: Google Ads · Meta (FB/Insta) Ads · Landing pages · Conversion tracking · A/B testing · Monthly reports.
- FAQ: "What's the minimum ad budget?" · "When will I see results?" · "Do you report transparently?"

### 5.5 Social Media Management
- H1: **A brand people remember — posted consistently.**
- Sub: Content, design, and community management that keep you visible and build trust, handled end-to-end.
- What we do: Content calendar · Reels/graphics · Copywriting · Community management · Influencer coordination · Monthly analytics.
